장지영

[tɕijʌŋ tɕaŋ] (she/her)


I'm a post-doctoral researcher at Hanyang Institute for Phonetics and Cognitive Sciences of Language (HIPCS). I received my PhD in Linguistics from University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB) in 2023. At UCSB, I was a member of the Speech, Prosody, and Articulatory Kinematics (SPArK) Laboratory. My current research projects involve L1 and L2 phonetics, phonetics-prosody interface, Articulatory Phonology, speech production/perception, and computational linguistics.
